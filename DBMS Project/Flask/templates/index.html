United Streaming!

We all love a good show and movie but we all hate a poor search function.

Here is some help ;).

Streaming services are the new age television, a source of entertainment for the 21st century. Unlike television, a streaming service has a large database to provide anything you want, advertisement free. The content on these services is considerably large and meets the tastes of various kinds of people. They are considerably affordable and hence a large mass uses these services. Our project is an add-on to these services to help the user.

Our project brings you an advanced search option improved which improves the streaming experience when integrated with streaming services. It also provides recommendations based on your previous entertainment endeavours and lets the user provide their feedback to help assess the content further for other user. You may also create your personalised watchlist to return to whenever, wherever.
